The Project

The goal of the SirenSeek project was to design a unique, fully functional website that would not only replace their existing WIX website with better findability, but would provide SirenSeek, LLC with new branding that could be used for marketing purposes.

My role was to singlehandedly redesign the website, including all visual content, and then develop the website using HTML, CSS, & JS. Development also included proper setup of SEO, which was not previously optimal.

Stakeholder requirements included: Company information, Job Portals, & contact forms. Another requirement was to opt for illustrative vector graphics over the use of stock imagery that would give the company a website that stood out from other recruitment firms.

The outcome after several months of design and development is a fully functional responsive website optimized for better findability, and a new visual look that is now used across SirenSeek, LLCâ€™s marketing collateral.
