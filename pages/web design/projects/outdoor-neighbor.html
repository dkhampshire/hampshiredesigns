UX/UI Prototypes For a Wildlife Viewing App

The Project

The goal of Outdoor Neighbor was to create an app that would allow users to post and view animal sighting locations on a community map. Target users consists of wildlife enthusiasts, hikers, backpackers, and nature lovers who could use the map to view animal sightings before trekking through an area or post sightings on their excursions. Users would be able to view or post information such as animal type, safety level, animal behavior, photos, and more!

User needs included:

  • Posting sightings
  • Taking Photos
  • Navigating the community map
  • Searching for sightings
  • Filtering by various criteria
  • Following & commenting
  • Sharing to Social Media


Tools: Adobe XD, Photoshop, Inkscape, Google Earth
